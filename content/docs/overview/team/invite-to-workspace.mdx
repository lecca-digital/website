---
title: Invite to Workspace
---

Inviting team members will give them access to your workspace connections, knowledge, and variables.

<Callout type="info">
  On the **Starter** or **Professional** plans, you can only have one team
  member in your workspace. Upgrade to the **Team** plans to invite additional
  members.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
  If you are hosting Lecca.io, you'll need a commercial license to enjoy this
  feature.
</Callout>

<Steps>
<Step>

### Open Invitation Form

To invite a team member to your workspace, navigate to the Team page and click on the `Invite` button

</Step>

<Step>
### Enter Email

Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.

</Step>

<Step>
### Choose Role

Choose their role from the dropdown menu. You can choose from **Maintainer** or **Member**.

</Step>

</Steps>

<Callout>
  If your team needs access to project-specific components, you'll need to
  invite them to those specific projects separately.
</Callout>
